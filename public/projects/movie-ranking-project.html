The Best Movies According to Shefali

My top 3 movies of all-time.

The Chronicles Of Narnia

This movie encapsulates it's viewers into a world beyond our vision, allowing our imagination to run wild, solidifying our faith and belief in the strength of collaboration and unity between all things living.

Precious

Precious is the story of a young woman facing real life problems, and a film many young women can resonate with. We all struggle with the pressures of adolesence, some more than others, and this movie is an empowering one everyone should take the time to watch.

Fall

Thrillers, are just that! Thrillers, and this movie will leave you hanging off the edge of your seat.
